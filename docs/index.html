SERVAL FITNESS

So what is it?

Serval is a fitness app that allows you to track your workouts, log your progress, ensure you meet your fitness goals with a robust community driven reward system.

Setting reccords.

Earn rewards as you reach new milestones, set new records and explore new activities.

Global leader-board.

The global leader-board is a ranking system that puts you up against other user to see how you compete in your sport/s of choice. The leader-board is divided into tiers: newbie, bronze, silver, gold, platinum, diamond and finally apex-athletes.
